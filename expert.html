Expert

Expert is an app designed to seamlessly link users seeking specialized knowledge with certified Experts in their respective fields. The Expert serves as a trustworthy source, ensuring users receive accurate and dependable answers to their questions. Whether it's professional advice, technical know-how, or in-depth insights, Expert connects you with qualified individuals who are equipped to provide the precise information you need.

My Process

Research & Insights

I started with a competitive analysis to determine what products are currently available to users and where the gaps are in those products. The analysis lead me to an idea of how the market could be improved and gave me a launch pad to start reaching out to potential users and conduct research on what they found useful and relevant.

Here were my research goals:

  • To understand how people currently solve their questions and if they read expert advice.
  • Help define topics or categories that users would find useful research subjects.
  • Determine pain points when users attempt to answer their questions using their own research methods.
  • Gauge users pros and cons about a flexible pricing structure similar to AirBnb.
  • Understand how people commonly solve problems that they do not have answers to.
  • Learn what features a user of the app might like to see.

From my research I was able to gain these insights:

  • Users think that a native live chat/video feature is an imperative function of the app.
  • Not only is it important for the user to be able to find an Expert easily, Experts need the ability to understand the specifics of the user's question to eliminate unnecessary back and forth.
  • The interviewees found that there was room and use for a subscription based plan and a flexible pricing structure.
  • There are various methods for doing research without the aid of an expert, all of which can leave the user's question unanswered if the question is very specific.
  • An app/service similar to this is not commonly used, but there were a few similar apps or services that interviewees have heard of, some being good examples to review for ideas.
  • Several good ideas were shared by interviewees, specifically around what types of services they would find useful offered by an Expert.

Design

Expert has started to take shape. Wireframes are coming together and the vision for what the app could be is starting to come to life all while keeping my 3 user personas in mind. Now that I have some of the experiences designed and in a working condition, I did some more testing.

Usability tests were conducted to help me determine if I've judged my audience correctly. Wireframes started to elevate to high fidelity and as I worked on my designs, I ran preference tests. A quick check in with my audience gave me the confidence to move forward with certain designs based on the results.

While starting the process of building the high fidelity wireframes, I introduced new design principles. These principles started to make the unfinished and unfamiliar UI start to feel cohesive and familiar.
